Get to know me!

I am a first-year astronomy graduate student at The Ohio State University. I am a first-generation hispanic woman studying astrophysics. I am interested in neutron star mergers, dark matter's distribution in dwarf galaxies, and most recently supernovae progenitors. My pronouns are she/her. I am orginally from Northern California. I completed my B.S. in Physics, specializing in astrophysics at the University of California, Irvine in 2021. I love going on camping trips and stargazing. I enjoy playing tennis and going on walks, and especially if it's a coffee walk.
